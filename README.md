# SMART-INVENTORY-AND-SALES-ANALYTICS-SYSTEM
## Overview
The Smart Inventory & Sales Analytics System is a web-based application designed to simplify inventory, customer, vendor, and sales management. It provides separate panels for Admin and Executive roles, ensuring secure and efficient operations.
## Features
#🔹 Product & Inventory Management

Add, update, and delete products with full CRUD functionality.

Track stock levels and generate alerts for low inventory.

Export product data to Excel for reporting.

#🔹 Customer & Sales Management

Register and manage customer details.

Record sales transactions and generate invoices.

Download invoices in PDF format.

Track customer purchases and sales history.

#🔹 Vendor & Purchase Management

Manage vendor details and purchase orders.

Place purchase requests for required stock.

Notify vendors via email for product supply.

Vendors can deliver products to customers directly or to the warehouse.

#🔹 Email Notifications

Admin receives stock updates via email.

Vendors are informed through email about purchase requests.

Customers can be notified about order status.

#🔹 Analytics & Reporting

View total stock availability.

Profit and loss tracking with detailed reports.

Sales dashboards with charts and pivot tables.
## Tech Stack

Frontend: JSP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Backend: Java, MySQL

Data Visualization: Chart libraries, pivot tables

Email Integration: JavaMail API

🔹 Role-Based Panels

Admin Panel: Full control over products, customers, vendors, and analytics.
## Benefits

Streamlined inventory and sales management.

Automated invoice generation and email notifications.

Improved decision-making with analytics.

Secure role-based access for Admin and Executives.

Executive Panel: Limited access for managing sales and customer interactions.
## Example Workflow

Admin adds products and vendors.

Executive records customer sales and generates invoices.

System updates stock levels and sends notifications.

Admin reviews analytics for profit/loss and stock reports.

Vendors are informed via email to supply products.
